pygmin.mindist.alignRotation¶

pygmin.mindist.alignRotation(XA, XB)[source]¶

Align structure XB with XA

Align structure XB to be as similar as possible to structure XA. To be precise, rotate XB, so as to minimize the distance |XA - XB|.

Rotations will be done around the origin, not the center of mass
